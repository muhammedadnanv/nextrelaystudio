About NextRelay Studio

NextRelay Studio is a Relay.app Certified Partner engineering autonomous AI teams that replace manual workflows with intelligent, stateful automation systems. Founded by Muhammed, we started just one month ago with a mission to build the most efficient digital workforce for every business.

Our Mission

To design automation systems that save time and unlock scale. Every workflow we build reduces human workload, eliminates repetitive tasks, and transforms operations into a self-driven, error-resistant machine.

Our Vision

We imagine a future where businesses run with precision and clarity, powered by autonomous AI agents that collaborate seamlessly with human teams. Our goal is to build AI infrastructure that grows with your organization instead of creating simple task-based automations.

Meet the Founder

NextRelay Studio was founded by Muhammed, a tech contractor passionate about automation, system design, and AI-driven operations. With hands-on experience building high-impact workflows, he created NextRelay Studio to help businesses operate with the efficiency of a modern AI-powered enterprise.
